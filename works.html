WAKE_UP

UI & INFORMATION DESIGN / 2025

Wake_Up is an academic project that explores how conspiracy theories and misinformation spread in contemporary society. Focusing on Italy, where distrust of science and the media remains significant, the project examines conspiracy thinking as a psychological defense mechanism that simplifies complex realities. Typical cognitive patterns include: overestimation of coincidences, attribution of intentional connections to unlikely events, and poor analytical thinking.

12.9% OPENLY DISTRUST SCIENCE, AS SHOWN BY BELIEFS AROUND FLAT EARTH THEORIES AND THE MOON LANDING HOAX.

These data reflect a society where irrationality provides fertile ground for conspiracy thinking. Through the use of AI (ChatGPT), fake news content was generated to replicate the dynamics of disinformation. This research was then translated into a dark, conspiratorial visual identity, an immersive UX/UI design, and information design strategies, brought together in the creation of a blog-website that illustrates the relationship between data, narrative, and perception.

UNSEEN

UX/UI DESIGN & DATA RESEARCH / 2025

Unseen is a multidisciplinary project that raises awareness around social isolation and the human need for belonging, exploring two contrasting perspectives: the fear of missing out (FOMO) and voluntary withdrawal (hikikomori). The narrative unfolds through illustration and storytelling, blending two seemingly opposite experiences into a single immersive work that balances the visual with the introspective. “Unseen” captures the essence of the project: highlighting two conditions that are often overlooked or misunderstood by society, remaining “invisible.” The name is paired with a tagline “All eyes on me” that contrasts this invisibility, emphasizing the social pressure and judgment felt by those who experience it.

ONE PROJECT AND THREE INTERCONNECTED PARTS.

An illustrated book portrays the FOMO experience through a silent, grayscale narrative with a dark, immersive atmosphere. A personal diary explores hikikomori using a stream-of-consciousness approach, where words and spontaneous sketches trace the journey from initial discomfort to complete isolation. Augmented reality experiences allow readers to step into the obsessive dynamics of social media, culminating in a symbolic, screen-free conclusion. Finally, a dedicated website presents data visualizations and in-depth content, making the hidden dimensions of social anxiety visible and accessible to a broader audience.

TANK

BRAND IDENTITY & UX/UI DESIGN / 2024

Tank is an academic project developed to create a dynamic brand identity and a recognizable, modern icon system, designed to adapt across digital and print platforms while maintaining visual consistency and communicative flexibility. For creatives, enthusiasts, or beginners looking to explore analog photography and add a personal touch to everyday life. Tank celebrates the creative process through film cameras and manual development, telling a story of patience and dedication where time and imperfection capture memories and make moments eternal. Through interactive exhibitions, workshops, and hands-on experiences, the project invites people to rediscover the true essence of photography.

TANK’S MISSION IS TO REVIVE ANALOG PHOTOGRAPHY WHILE CELEBRATING EVERYDAY LIFE THROUGH EVENTS AND MEANINGFUL CONNECTIONS.

The Tank website promotes and supports the Tank app, which allows users to join photo exhibitions, explore stories through augmented reality, and share their own memories via the TankBox, our special photography challenge.

AWARENESS CAMPAIGNS

WOMEN SAY NO / GRAPHIC / 2024

The graphic concept focuses on a complete restyling of the Centro Donna Lilith’s visual identity for its membership campaign. Geometric shapes interlock to create a dynamic landscape, symbolizing the Center’s constant commitment—day and night—to providing a strong, accessible service for all women in need. Through tailored support pathways, the CAV offers survivors of gender-based violence the chance to reclaim their lives. The concept also highlights the Center’s transfeminist identity, which is expressed each year through initiatives that raise awareness and promote social change. The 2024 campaign, launched under the claim “Against violence, I stand with them”, invites people to join the cause by becoming members and actively supporting the Center’s mission.

IN ADDITION, AN AWARENESS CAMPAIGN WAS ALSO DEVELOPED TO ADDRESS VIOLENCE AGAINST WOMEN.

Its goal was to inform and encourage women in the community to seek help by turning to the local CAV for support. Visually, the campaign included posters and print materials designed to ensure effective communication across different media.

HYSTERIA / GRAPHIC CONTENT / 2023

WITH HYSTERIA WE RECLAIM OUR ANGER.

Hysteria is a project developed in collaboration with Centro Donna Lilith, an anti-violence center active in the Latina area. The feminist association organized a series of events to mark November 25th, the International Day for the Elimination of Violence Against Women. For the campaign, a range of materials were designed, including posters, social media content, and flyers to promote the events. The naming “Hysteria”, conceived by the association, inspired a bold visual identity—one that screams and channels women’s anger against the cultural issue of gender-based violence.
